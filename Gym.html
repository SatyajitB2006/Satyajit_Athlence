About This Gym

PowerHouse Gym is a premium fitness facility located in the heart of Pune City. With over 15,000 square feet of training space, we offer state-of-the-art equipment, expert trainers, and a variety of classes to help you achieve your fitness goals. Our facility features a full-size swimming pool, sauna, and dedicated areas for strength training, cardio, and functional fitness.

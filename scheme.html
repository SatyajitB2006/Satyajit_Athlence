Government Schemes for Athletes

Our platform connects you with the latest government initiatives to support your athletic journey. Find the perfect program to elevate your career.

National Sports Talent Scholarship

Funding

Financial support for promising athletes aged 12-25 to cover training, equipment, and competition expenses. Monthly stipend up to ₹50,000 based on performance.

Deadline: 15 Dec 2023 Apply Now

Elite Athlete Training Program

Training

World-class coaching and facilities for national-level athletes. Includes sports science support, nutrition planning, and international exposure opportunities.

Ongoing Enrollment Learn More

Para Athlete Development Fund

Funding

Specialized support for para-athletes including adaptive equipment grants, accessibility modifications, and competition travel funding up to ₹2,00,000 annually.

Quarterly Applications Apply Now

Rural Sports Talent Hunt

Training

Identifying and nurturing talent from rural areas with residential training camps, equipment provisions, and scholarship opportunities for outstanding performers.

Women in Sports Initiative

Funding

Special grants and mentorship programs for female athletes, addressing gender-specific challenges in sports participation and career development.

Deadline: 30 Nov 2023 Apply Now

Olympic Preparation Grant

Funding

Comprehensive support for athletes preparing for Olympic Games, including international training camps, coaching, and performance analysis support.

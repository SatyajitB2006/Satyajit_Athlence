Frequently Asked Questions

What sports can I train with VR?

Our platform currently supports basketball, soccer, tennis, baseball, golf, boxing, martial arts, and track sports. We're continuously adding new sports based on demand and technological advancements. Even if your sport isn't listed, many cognitive and reaction training modules are universally beneficial.

How does VR training compare to real practice?

VR training complements traditional practice by offering scenarios impossible to replicate physically. It enhances decision-making, reaction time, and mental visualization while reducing physical strain. Studies show VR training can accelerate skill acquisition by up to 40% when combined with physical practice.

What equipment do I need to bring?

Just wear comfortable athletic clothing and shoes. We provide all VR equipment, sanitized between each use. For certain sports like golf or baseball, you may want to bring your own glove or specialized footwear, but it's not required.

Is VR training safe for children?

Yes, we offer specialized programs for athletes aged 10+. Sessions are supervised by trained professionals, with adjusted intensity and duration for younger users. All equipment is properly fitted, and we include frequent breaks to prevent eye strain or discomfort.

Can VR help with injury rehabilitation?

Absolutely. Our rehabilitation programs are designed with physical therapists to safely rebuild strength, mobility, and confidence post-injury. VR provides real-time biofeedback and creates engaging environments that make repetitive exercises more motivating and measurable.
